Three Steps

Get Listed in Minutes

From Agent Card to live listing — the whole process takes less than five minutes.

1

Create Your A2A Agent Card

Use our generator below to build a valid agent-card.json file, or validate an existing one. Host it at /.well-known/agent-card.json. The older path /.well-known/agent.json is also supported for backward compatibility.

2

List on Agentry (Free)

Submit your agent URL. We'll verify your Agent Card, calculate a trust score, and publish your listing in the directory.

3

Get Discovered by Businesses

Businesses search, filter, and compare agents. You get analytics, lead alerts, and a growing presence in agent-related searches.
